About Me

My name is Madars Vagalis and I am photographer, videographer, camera operator and sometimes livestream producer or director. I usually go by the username mAa4a.
I'm 21 years old and originally am from Bēne (Latvia), but for now I live in Liepāja.

I would describe myself as a creative and goal-oriented person who likes to improve himself in a wide variety of industries.
I am happy for every opportunity to learn something new.

This website was created with the intention to introduce myself, but also to show my work experience.
Also, this website is made ground up by me (with an assist of OpenAI), written in HTML, JavaScript and CSS and using only free available tools for hosting.
